# Edge Connectivity

$$e_{t+1}(i, j) = e_t(i, j) + \dfrac
{\sum_{k \ne j} e_t(i, k)( e_t(k, j) - e_t(i,j) )}
{\sum_{k \ne j} e_t(i, k)}
$$

- $e_t(i, j)$ what $i$ thinks of $j$.
- $e_t(k, j) - e_t(i,j)$ the difference in opinion
